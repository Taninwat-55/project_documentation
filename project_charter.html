Road to Frontend Developer Project

Date: 18 May 2024

Document Status: Draft

Executive Summary:

This project aims to transform me into a competent frontend developer within three months. By adhering to the SMART framework, I will undertake a structured learning path, complete practical projects, and secure a job as a frontend developer by the end of August.

Project Goal

  • Acquire Core Skills
    • To have a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Learn a frontend framework/library (e.g., React, Bootstrap, etc.)
  • Build a Portfolio:
    • Develop at least three web development projects
    • Deploy projects to showcase technical abilities
  • Professional Development
    • Create and optimize a professional resume and LinkedIn profile
    • Prepare and practice for job interviews
  • Job Applications
    • Apply to at least 20 frontend developer job postings
    • Secure a job offer or reach the final interview stage

Deliverables

  • Completed online courses in frontend development.
  • A portfolio of at least three web development projects.
  • A professional resume and LinkedIn profile optimized for frontend development roles.
  • Applications to at least 20 frontend developer job postings.
  • Participation in at least two mock interviews.

Background

I am pursuing this project to transition into a career in frontend development to achieve a better financial situation and contribute to household expenses. By becoming a frontend developer, I aim to leverage the high demand in the tech industry to secure a well-paying job.

Benefits, Costs, and Budget

Benefits

  • Gain employable skills in a high-demand field.
  • Financial independence and ability to contribute to household expenses.
  • Personal and professional growth.

Costs

  • Coursera subscription: $59 (already spent) + $29 (extension)
  • Udemy courses: $50 (already spent)

Budget

  • Remaining budget: $50 for additional learning resources or tools.

Scope and Exclusion

In-Scope

  • Learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frontend frameworks like React.
  • Building and deploying web projects.
  • Developing a professional online presence.
  • Job application and interview preparation.

Out-of-Scope

  • Learning backend development.
  • In-depth study of non-frontend-specific technologies.
